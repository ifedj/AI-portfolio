02, AI Product

Lola AI

A conversational women's health companion built on WhatsApp, using a retrieval-first RAG architecture trained on proprietary Nigerian women's lab data.

Product

95% Accuracy, Menstrual Cycle Prediction

RAG Architecture · WhatsApp · Proprietary Dataset

60% of Healthtracka users were women. We had 100K+ proprietary lab data points sitting idle with no existing model trained on African women's health data. I conducted 100 user interviews and discovered women didn't want another app, they had feature phones, limited storage, and trusted WhatsApp already.

Architectural decision: retrieval-first RAG with strict guardrails. The system retrieves; the LLM presents. It never invents. This constraint was critical in a high-stakes health context where hallucination rates in 2023 were unacceptable.

3-Layer Evaluation Framework

L1
Clinician Review Rolling doctor review to catch boundary violations, e.g. flagging pregnancy test recommendations for women one day late on their period.
L2
Behavioral Outcome Tracking Adherence as north star metric. A technically accurate recommendation nobody follows has zero health value. Retention improved 20% after discovering users valued timely nudges over biomarker accuracy.
L3
Safety Kill Switch Automatic block for outputs crossing diagnostic or medication thresholds. Triggered by output category classification combined with confidence scoring.
